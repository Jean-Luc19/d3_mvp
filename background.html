If data is the new oil, water just might be the newer, oilier oil.

Water wars are a real possibility in our lifetime

  • 783 million people do not have access to clean and safe water worldwide.1
  • 319 million people in Sub-Saharan Africa are without access to improved reliable drinking water sources.2
  • The water crisis is the #1 global risk based on impact to society (as a measure of devastation), as announced by the World Economic Forum in January 2015.3
  • It takes 2,500 gallons of water to produce one hamburger.
